Killer inc. was a Brooklyn-based design consultancy and product company. We had over a decade of experience designing complex software and service design systems. Founded in 2014 by partners Brandy Bora and Jennifer Williams, the pair met several years ago while working at a design consulting firm, frog design in New York City. The two and bonded over a passion of pursuit for highly effective design methods and working with technology to design useful tools for people.
