We create things that are fun and useful.

 
 

Killer inc. is a Brooklyn-based design consultancy and product company. We have over a decade of experience designing complex software and service design systems. Founded in 2014 by partners Brandy Bora and Jennifer Williams, the pair met several years ago while working at a design consulting firm, frog design in New York City. The two and bonded over a passion of pursuit for highly effective design methods and working with technology to design useful tools for people.

Who We Are
 

Brandy Bora

I am a lead designer at American Express in the Enterprise Growth Group which focuses on services for the underbanked. Before joining American Express, I was an interaction designer and design researcher at frog Design in New York where I worked on a variety of future vision projects as well as enterprise applications. I studied industrial design and design management at the graduate level at SCAD. Prior to Graduate school I worked for my family's project and facility management firm as a project assistant while attending UGA where I studied theatrical design, fibers, and Japanese traditional art and language. (I make future shit and enterprise and now bank stuff)

 
 

Jennifer Williams

I entered design through illustration and animation in my early years. After realizing programming allowed me to do things at a greater capacity, I decided to explore the realm interface design by working with Flash. In 2007, after completing my design studies at Parsons Design + Technology MFA program, I discovered that I had a great passion for understanding how humans are connected to technology. I joined up at frog NY in 2009 where I met Brandy and a bunch of other amazing people, and also where I was fortunate to also get the chance to work on a number of enterprise software and service design projects, some future vision pieces, as well as a custom mobile OS for a touch-screen trading turret. I now am working in the educational technology space, and am currently UX Director for the Amplify tablet product. We are defining an integrated 1:1 solution for K-8 classrooms.
