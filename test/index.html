We like design to be empowering, engagingly elegant, and enduring above all.

 
 

Killer inc. is a Brooklyn-based design consultancy and product company. We have over a decade of experience designing complex software and service design systems. Founded in 2014 by partners Brandy Bora and Jennifer Williams, the pair met several years ago while working at a design consulting firm, frog design in New York City. The two and bonded over a passion of pursuit for highly effective design methods and working with technology to design opportunistic tools for people.

Who We Are

 

Brandy Bora

I am a lead designer at American Express in the Enterprise Growth Group which focuses on services for the underbanked. Before joining American Express, I was an interaction designer and design researcher at frog Design in New York where I worked on a variety for projects for UNICEF, GE, and Comcast. I studied industrial design and design management at the graduate level at SCAD. Prior to Graduate school I worked for my family's project and facility management firm as a project assistant while attending UGA where I studied theatrical design, fibers, and Japanese traditional art and language.

 
 

Jennifer Williams

I am an experience and interaction designer. I work in a strategic and innovative fashion to orchestrate how people interact with digital and analog experiences over time. I enjoy taking a look at software and finding out how to present it in a more human fashion. I have a unique background in the sense that I have programming, interaction design and visual design skills. This has strengthened my abilities as a digital product designer. It has allowed me to understand on a deeper level how software is built and be involved in the entire process of it's creation.
